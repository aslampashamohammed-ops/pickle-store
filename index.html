❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are your pickles 100% organic?

Yes! All our pickles are made with 100% organic, non-GMO ingredients sourced directly from trusted farmers. No artificial preservatives, colors, or chemicals.

How long do pickles last?

Our pickles are made with traditional preservation methods and have a shelf life of 1-2 years when stored in cool, dry places. Once opened, consume within 2-3 months for best quality.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary. Contact our support team for specific queries about your location.

Can I return or exchange pickles?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied. Unopened jars can be exchanged for different flavors. Please contact us within 7 days of delivery.

Are there any allergens in your pickles?

Our pickles contain mustard oil and spices. Some varieties may contain peanuts or tree nuts. Please check individual product descriptions for allergen information or contact us directly.
