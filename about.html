Our Story

Preserving tradition, bringing quality to your table since 1998

🥒 The Pickle Store Legacy

Founded in 1998, Pickle Store started as a small family operation with a simple mission: bring authentic, homemade Indian pickles to every household across the country. What began in a small kitchen in Delhi has now grown into a trusted brand served in homes nationwide.

Our founder, Mrs. Rajeshwari Sharma, inherited generations of pickle-making knowledge from her grandmother. Today, her grandchildren carry forward this legacy with the same passion and dedication to quality and authenticity.

Every jar of pickle is made with love, using recipes passed down through four generations, premium organic ingredients, and traditional preparation methods that ensure authentic taste and nutrition.
